Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Write for You?

Do you have a passion for music? Wading into the sea of music journalism can be daunting. The good news is we are always looking for exciting new talent. Submit your story and photos to rhondahz70@gmail.com

Do you accept concert photos from your readers?

Photos are accepted from all photographers internationally. All processes, formats, techniques and experience levels are welcomed. Please follow guidelines closely. Images are chosen by artistic vision, and overall excellence. All are subjective...

Why didn't you review [insert name here]'s latest disc?

Literally hundreds of new albums are released each week and arrive at Unplugged's offices, all seeking print and online reviews and/or mentions. It is physically impossible to cover all of them within the magazine or online. Billboard's experts choose various titles to review based on retail and airplay potential, as well as artistic merit.

You made a mistake/I know more than you/you left something out of your article.

Although none of those are questions, unplugged.com readers often have comments and/or information regarding articles that are published on the site. If you feel you can correct or add something significant, please direct your comments to the site's editorial staff via the links on the "Contact Us" page.
